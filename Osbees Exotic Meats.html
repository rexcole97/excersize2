Who are we?

Osbees Exotic Meat resturaunt was founded by the Osbee family in 1965. Doc Osbee determined that the dining expereince had died down and something new and exciting must change the inustry. Starting off with ostrich burgers Doc was able to captivate the minds and mouths of foodgoers and established a brick and mortar resturaunt that solidified their place in the resturaunt industry.
